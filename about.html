This platform is powered by collaboration, combining accurate scholarship with passionate exploration. We recognize four key user roles that drive our content and interaction:

Admin (The Curator)

Manages the platform, ensures content accuracy and quality control, oversees user interactions, and maintains the technical integrity of the site.

Cultural Enthusiast (The Explorer)

Our core user. Explores content, participates in virtual tours, engages in cultural discussions, and discovers the heritage of India.

Content Creator (The Educator)

Develops and updates educational articles, crafts detailed site descriptions, creates interactive features, and curates virtual tour material.

Tour Guide (The Storyteller)

Conducts live virtual tours, provides real-time historical insights, shares local knowledge, and answers user queries during scheduled events.
